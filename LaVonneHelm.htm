Update 2022:

Greetings! Since the last posting……5 years REALLY! Mike and I moved to Pebble Creek…a 55 and older community in Goodyear Arizona. We hit our 53rd anniversary this year. We are living our best retirement life. I belong to the quilt group, gardening group, play MahJongg, and walk. Mike plays golf a LOT! We are still traveling. Our son and family are still in Alabama. Happy class reunion!

LaVonne Helm Christensen

Update 2017:

Here is my story. I went to beauty school in Des Moines. Married Mike Christensen from Ogden and off we went on a 25 year adventure in the Air Force. We moved every 2-3 years living around the world and all over the US. Mike started a second career in the Washington DC area where we lived for 15 years. We retired to Colorado 7 years ago...BUT....we we are moving one last time we hope. Colorado has been colder than we like so moving to a retirement community in Arizona this summer.

We have one son, his wife, and granddaughter living in Alabama. I spend time sewing , gardening, d ecorating homes, walking and we travel. Have fun!
