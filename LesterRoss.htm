Started WIUBOG program.
1984 2nd son Born Dustin (Civil Engineer, Furrier, Horse Trainer Edgemont SD).
1989 3rd Son Born Nathan Soon to be Accountant. Upper Iowa. (2008) co-captian on the Boone High Baseball, Football, and Track Teams)

Graduated WIUBOG with Honors in Sociology an Military Science.
2000 Retired From Iowa National Guard.

For 10 years I worked for Genesis Development Boone Iowa as a vocational work supervisor for handicapped adults.
I now do volunteer work for RSVP respite care, Boone County Freedom Flight, an Service Officer for the Boone VFW.
