In Memorium

The Classmates on this page are no longer with us. Even though they are gone, their memory will be with us forever. Perhaps one of these classmates was your best friend, or someone who touched your life in a very special way. We remember their smile, their laugh, or passing them in the hall of Boone High School. We shared so much during our growing up years in high school, and we hope these obituaries will not be the final chapter. Words and newspaper clippings can never replace our memories and heartfelt feelings. Please remember them in prayer and wish them well. We were all part of the class of "1967."
