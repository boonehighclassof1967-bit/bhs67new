After our high school graduation I began a wonderful career with what was then known as the AT&T/Bell system. My first assignment was in Albany, Georgia, and from there I was transferred to many different cities including Ames, Dubuque, Waterloo, Des Moines, Seattle and Phoenix. When the new millennium approached I decided I needed to also find a new approach to my life. So I made the decision to retire. It was the best decision I ever made! I have found I have so much more diversity and flexibility in my life now. I can travel wherever and whenever I want, trifle with my new career in real estate, play golf, tend to my rose garden, scuba dive, volunteer at a local domestic violence shelter, take yoga lessons, entertain family and friends and discover new things every day. Life is good!!!
