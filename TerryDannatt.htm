Update 2017:
2010 retired from Iowa state University and began full time House Director positions traveling the United States.

Ames, IA
Terry works as an Administrative Assistant at ISU Design Services since 1992.

I lived in Southern Iowa and Oklahoma until I moved back to Boone in 1992. I have two daughters: Lisa DeFever who lives in Overland Park, KS and Aime' DeVore, Ft. Lauderdale FL. Alan DeFever is Lisa's husband and they gave me my first grandson, Maxwell French DeFever, who is the center of my universe.

My mom, Mae Dannatt, still lives in Boone and was voted Mother of Class of 1967 at the last class reunion. My brother Allan is in Houston, TX with his family, Barb, Jason & Christopher. My brother Marty lives in Johnston and has two daughters, Kelly & Kaci.
