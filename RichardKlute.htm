In Memory[August 15, 2004]
(Obituary)
The Boundary Waters in 1977 - We made our trip north in the fall. We encountered rain and cold weather. The attached pic shows Rick warming himself by our campfire. Had it not been raining I think we would have started a forest fire. Rick decided to use "go juice" to get the fire going. I think he was smiling because we dodged a big bullet. Our hearts were pounding until the flames died down.
