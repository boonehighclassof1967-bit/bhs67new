BHS 1967 Class Member

Jacque Furman


1967

1967

2026 Update: Lives in Guthrie, OK

Jacque moved to L.A. where she played with the first all female rock bands, "Fanny". Later she joined the San Francisco soul band "Cold Blood" with Lydia Pense. During that time she was offered a gig with, blues guitarist, Albert King, but she decided to stay with Cold Blood. She later toured with "Glenn Yarborough and the Limeliters". He called her "The Goddess of Rhythm". She later performed with the band "Pure Love and Pleasure" in New York City with the Earthlight Theatre Group.
