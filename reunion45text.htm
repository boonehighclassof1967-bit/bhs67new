GOING BACK TO THE SIXTIES IN OUR 60'S!

Plans are coming together for our class party - here's what we know so far:

Friday, July 13, 2012 - LET'S HAVE A PICNIC!
McHose Park Shelter House
We've rented the shelter house from 3:00pm-11:00pm. We'll be grilling hamburgers and hotdogs at 6:30, but will have finger foods to nibble on earlier. We are asking people to bring appetizers or desserts (HyVee has a great selection!). Hopefully, Tim Aspengren will, once again, provide his rolling bar and we'll provide beer/wine/pop. We will have "our" music playing in the background - bring along your favorite CDs if you want. If we feel like turning the shelter house into "GRID", we'll move the tables and turn up the music! Since the park wants us out of there by 11:00pm, we'll announce where the after-party will be taking place.

Saturday, July 14, 2012 - 9:30am: Golf Outing
COME ON OUT TO CEDAR POINTE GOLF COURSE!
(this is the former Boone Country Club)

5:00pm - ???: We will gather for cocktails and hors d'oeuvres followed by dinner. We are putting a program together and CDs will provide background music, but if the urge hits.... we'll dance!
