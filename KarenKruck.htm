Update 2012:
After high school I went to Simpson College for one year, then graduated from AIB, and after working a few years went back to college to get a teaching degree. I have retired from teaching after 33 years, and have been married almost 42 years now. Jim was in banking for 26 years before we started DeVore Homes, which builds custom system built homes.

We have 6 grandchildren now, and enjoy them so much! Jennifer and Tom live in Clive and have a 7 year old, and a 3 month old. Heather and Ross live in Davenport and have a 6 year old, and a 2 year old. Stephanie and Chad live in West Des Moines and have a 2 year old, and a 5 month old.

I haven’t been to a class reunion for a while, and look forward to being able to finally make this one.
