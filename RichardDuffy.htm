2022 Update

Retired & still living in Boone. My wife, Jeanne, and I celebrated 53 yrs. together in March and 49 yrs. as a married couple in May. We have a daughter and a son. We're expecting our first grandchild early next year. Pray for us. For those who had Mrs. Heaps latin class, Ora pro nobis - yep, some BHS learnin' stuck. Since retiring I've got two new knees. Still can't dance. Elaine of Seinfeld has nothing on me. One, two, what? Covid blunted any kind of post-retirement euphoria. But we're getting into the swing of it lately with visits to family in Washington D.C., Virginia Beach, and Charlotte N.C.. I'm attaching some relatively recent family pictures.
