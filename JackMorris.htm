MORRIS, JACK; BOONE. KIA Korea DMZ, 1969/10/18 Boone High School Class of 1967.
Jack had enlisted in the United States Army. Served during the Vietnam War. Morris had the rank of Specialist Four. Attached to 1st Battalion, 32nd Infantry Regiment, 7th Infantry Division, Company C .Specialist Morris was killed when North Korean infiltrators ambushed his truck in the Demilitarized Zone (DMZ) on October 18, 1969.
