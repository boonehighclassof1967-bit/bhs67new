BHS 1967 Class Member

Judi Webb


1967

Address: Boone, IA 50036
Occupation: Librarian
Life History: Where should I begin - Oh yeah - Graduation - PANIC! Too scared to go to college - too scared to find a job! So I babysat for years - from 1968 - 1978, I was taking care of my niece and nephew while my sister and her husband worked! Then there was the summer at Boone County Day Care - followed by eventually getting a part time job at the Ericson Public Library. I have been full time in the youth department (we used to call it the Children's room - remember Helen Stevens?) for 7 years. I am a member of Cornerstone Alliance Church and Boone Community Theatre. That's all, folks!
