Update 2010:
Terry Dannatt said, "God took Sheri with him this morning @12.30 am, 12/14/2010. Please let all Toreadors know that all email messages were received and I read them to her. I believe that she heard every word and I know she would be very grateful for all the kind, encouraging words sent from her beloved friends in the class of '67. She will be missed by her family and friends. Her daughter, Trishelle, and son, Travis, are making funeral arrangements today and I will update information as I get it. Sheri, Sheri BABY, we love you."

Update 2007:
I am still living in Spirit Lake. I have 4 grandchildren. Trishelle and her husband (Steve) live here (2 children) (Jessica and D. Jay) and Travis and Laura live in Phoenis with 2 children (Alaina and Sarah). Jessica received the President's scholarship to Gustavus Adolphus and she will start this year, which is quite an honor.
